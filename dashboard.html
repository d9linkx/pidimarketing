Terms of Service & Refund Eligibility

At PIDI Marketing, we strive to ensure 100% satisfaction for every campaign launched. However, because we initiate real-time human engagement immediately upon payment verification, our refund policy is strictly structured to protect both our performers and our clients.

1. Non-Performance: If your campaign fails to reach the target quantity of engagements within the specified timeframe (standard 72 hours unless otherwise stated), you are eligible for a pro-rated refund for the unfulfilled portion of the campaign.

2. Quality Standards: Every engagement is verified by our AI "Bot-Killer" and manual audit team. If you find engagements that do not meet our "Rule of 10 Words" or quality guidelines, please report them within 24 hours for a credit replacement or partial refund.

3. Direct Payment: Please note that payments made via bank transfer are for specific service delivery. Once performers are assigned and tasks are claimed, the funds are committed to the workforce pool to ensure fair compensation for our human taskers.

4. Cancellation: Campaigns can be paused at any time, but funds already distributed to performers for completed tasks cannot be reversed. Remaining balances can be applied to new campaigns or refunded upon request.

5. Support: To request a review of your campaign, please contact the Support Desk with your Transaction ID. Our team typically resolves all claims within 48 hours.
