FAQ

How do I track my task payment?

Go to your Wallet section in the Earn Feed. Payments move from 'Pending' to 'Available' within 24 hours of task verification.

I haven't received my traffic yet?

Organic human traffic delivery starts within 1-3 hours of order placement. If you don't see results after 6 hours, contact our Growth team.

Can I cancel my strategy subscription?

Yes, all marketing strategy plans can be managed via your dashboard. Cancellations require 7 days notice before the next billing cycle.

What happens if my account is locked?

If your account is flagged for review, please contact Troubleshooting support immediately to verify your human engagement status.

Is there live support available?

Yes, live chat is available for verified Power Earners and Enterprise Strategy clients during business hours.
