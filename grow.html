Got Questions?
We’ve Got Answers.

1. Is this just another "Bot" or "Click Farm" service?

Absolutely not. Bots and click farms use automation and direct links that leave digital footprints, which usually get accounts shadowbanned. We provide Human-Powered Engagement Velocity. We use real people who interact with your content through a proprietary discovery process. To protect our unique infrastructure and your account's safety, we only discuss the technical specifics during our Strategy Deep-Dive calls.

2. Will the platforms (Instagram, LinkedIn, X) flag my account?

Safety is our #1 priority. Because our method mirrors natural fan behavior—including "Search & Intent" signals—it is indistinguishable from organic viral growth. We don't use direct links, which is the main trigger for platform red flags. We've managed over 500+ accounts with a 0% penalty rate.

3. Can I choose what the comments say?

We don't do "copy-paste" comments. You can provide us with a "Sentiment Direction" (e.g., “Focus on my expertise in real estate” or “Ask about international shipping”). Our team then crafts unique, conversational responses that are at least 10 words long. This ensures the engagement looks—and feels—100% authentic.

4. How fast does the engagement start after I post?

Timing is everything in the "Viral Window." Depending on your plan, our team begins the discovery and engagement process within 15 to 60 minutes of your post going live. This early velocity is what signals to the algorithm that your content is "Hot" and needs to be pushed to the Explore page.

5. How do you find my posts without me sending you a link?

This is part of our Secret Sauce. We have developed a "Natural Entry" protocol that involves our team finding your content the same way a super-fan would. This removes the "Referral Trace" that most agencies leave behind. We’ll show you exactly how this looks during a private demo.

6. Does this work for "Boring" niches like B2B or SaaS?

"Boring" niches actually need us the most! We specialize in Deep Engagement. Instead of generic "Great post!" comments, we seed intellectual questions that allow you to reply and showcase your expertise. We turn your comment section into a high-level consulting floor.

7. Will this help me get more real organic followers?

Yes, but indirectly. We provide the Velocity and Social Proof. When the algorithm sees our engagement, it pushes your post to new people (non-followers). When those new people land on your post and see a bustling, intelligent conversation, they are 80% more likely to hit "Follow" than they would be on a silent post.

8. Why don't you show your full client list on the website?

Privacy is part of the service. Many of our clients are high-level founders and celebrities who need to maintain the "Magic" of their organic growth. We operate under strict NDA-level confidentiality. We are the secret engine; you are the face of the brand.

9. Can I use this for Ads or Sponsored posts?

Highly recommended. Using the PIDI "Viral Spark" on an ad post lowers your Cost Per Click (CPC). Platforms reward ads that have high engagement because it proves the ad is relevant to the audience. We help you get more "Bang" for your ad buck.

10. How do I know which plan is right for me?

Every brand has a different "Algorithm Health" score. Some need a "Trust Bridge" (Starter), while others need a "Viral Engine" (Pro). The best way to find out is to Book a 15-minute Strategy Call. We’ll audit your current reach and tell you exactly which protocol will break your specific "invisibility ceiling."
