FAQ

How long does verification take?

Most tasks are verified within 24 hours. Once our system confirms your comment length and screenshot, the funds move from pending to available.

Is there a limit to how many tasks I can do?

No! You can complete as many available tasks as you want. Our "Task Board" is updated in real-time as new brands join the platform.

Can I do this from my desktop?

While you can browse tasks on desktop, we highly recommend using a mobile device so you can take advantage of deep-linking directly into social apps.

What happens if my task is rejected?

If a task is rejected, it's usually because the 10-word limit wasn't met or the screenshot was unclear. You'll receive feedback and can often try a different task immediately.
